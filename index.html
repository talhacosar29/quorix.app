Features

Small lessons. Big habit.

No long courses — just a focused minute of learning you'll actually finish. Everything works without an account; your progress stays on your device.

👆

Swipe to learn

Swipe right when you've got it, left for later — or use the buttons below the card. Colorful animated cards match each topic.

📅

10 cards every day

Nine learning categories plus a headlines card. Fresh deck each day with a full year of rotating content.

🔥

Streak tracking

Mark every card as learned to complete the day and grow your streak. Optional daily reminders keep you consistent.

⏱️

Timed quiz

After your deck, reinforce learning with Easy (3 questions), Medium (5), or Hard (10) — each question on the clock.

🗂️

Category archives

Browse 400+ cards per topic in Explore. Preview the first 15 free; full archive access coming with Premium.

📰

Live headlines

A dedicated News tab with BBC and TRT RSS feeds. Read summaries in-app and open the original source.

🔖

Favorites & share

Save cards you love. Share as plain text or a branded 9:16 Story image — perfect for social.

🌙

Dark mode & languages

Switch between light and dark themes. Full Turkish and English UI with matching localized content.

🔒

Privacy-first

No sign-up, no cloud sync. Cards and archives work offline. News and ads need internet only when you use them.
